Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Short version: We collect your email to notify you when Kaku launches. We don't sell it, share it with advertisers, or use it for anything else. You can ask us to delete it anytime.

1. Who we are

Kaku ("we", "us", "our") is a Japanese writing learning app currently in development. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you sign up for our waitlist at kaku.app.

2. Information we collect

Information you give us

  • Email address — collected when you join our waitlist. Required to notify you when the app launches.

Information collected automatically

  • IP address — collected at the time of signup for fraud prevention and basic analytics.
  • Signup timestamp — to track when you joined the waitlist.

We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics tools on this website.

3. How we use your information

We use your email address solely to:

  • Send you a verification code to confirm your email address
  • Send you a welcome email confirming your waitlist spot
  • Notify you when Kaku launches on Android
  • Send occasional updates about the app (no more than 1–2 emails per month)

We will never sell, rent, or share your email address with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data storage and security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase (hosted on AWS in Asia-Pacific region). Supabase is SOC 2 compliant. Your email is stored in a protected database with row-level security enabled.

OTP (one-time password) codes expire after 10 minutes and are deleted after use.

We use industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS) for all data in transit.

5. Email service provider

We use Resend to send transactional emails (verification codes and welcome emails). Resend receives your email address solely to deliver these emails. View Resend's privacy policy at resend.com/privacy-policy.

6. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction — ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Deletion — request that we delete your email from our waitlist at any time
  • Opt-out — unsubscribe from our emails using the unsubscribe link in any email we send

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@kaku.app. We will respond within 7 days.

7. Children's privacy

Our waitlist is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify waitlist subscribers of significant changes via email. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent version.

9. Contact us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@kaku.app
  • Support page: kaku.app/support
